parsing.TopologyDict

parsing.TopologyDict

A raw representation of a topology file.

Every section, apart from ffdir and define, comes with a condition that can be checked against the defines by the helper functions to determine if the content (a list of lists) should come from content or else_content. Some sections such as moleculetype also come with subsections.
